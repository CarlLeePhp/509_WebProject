Clearing Custom

Shipping internationally requires your shipment to be inspected by Custom when entering the border of the country. It might cause you some delay or in the worst case your items might be returned to you. Generally there are three steps to clear the custom.
1. Learn about Prohibited goods

Learn about what you can't ship or what items are restricted in other country before sending your package.

2. Fill your Commercial invoice

fill your document and all necessary information that will describe or explain the purpose of your goods.

3. Pay your taxes / duties

Pay your taxes in advance . It's necessary for consumer to be aware on how much you have to pay when transferring your goods.
