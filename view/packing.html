Learn how to pack your items properly

1. Avoid empty space
Always fill empty space with foam or any solid items. So there will be no room for the items to move around that might cause damage to your items
2. Seal your boxes properly
Sealing your boxes properly with tape on each gap of the box.
Make sure the tape is long enough and the tape stick strongly to the surface of the box
Do a cross tape
3. Quality of the boxes
Choose a boxes that are strong and in good condition
